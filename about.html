kuyant pharmacourse


KUYANT PHARMACOURSE is a digital platform that gives both under-graduate and post-graduate pharmacy technicians, the comprehensive, specialized, simulation, theoretical and practical education they need to achieve entry to practice competencies as outlined by the National Association of Pharmacy Regulatory Authorities (NAPRA).

Do you know that: Pharmacy technicians ensure the safety and quality of prescription-product preparation.

They also collaborate with pharmacists and health professionals in health and wellness promotion, disease prevention and chronic disease management in order to support patients in optimizing health goals.

The course therefore utilizes innovative delivery methods to assist the various learners in acquiring the most up to date knowledge and practices.

Courses are delivered through a combination of online video and audio lectures, labs, and hybrid processes.
